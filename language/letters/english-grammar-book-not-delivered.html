Model Formal Letter

Letter 15: English Grammar Book Not Delivered

Structure Highlights
Format: Formal | Word Count: 194 Words | Reading Time: ~1 mins | Standard: ICSE Board Exam
Sender's Address
34, Lake View Road
Kolkata – 700029
Date
8 July 2026
Recipient's Address
To
The Manager
ABC Online Book Store
Kolkata – 700001
Subject Line
Subject: Non-delivery of English Grammar book
Salutation
Sir,
Body Paragraph

I am writing to bring to your notice the delay in the delivery of an English Grammar book that I ordered from your online bookstore.

Body Paragraph

The order was placed on 25 June 2026, and according to the delivery schedule, the book was expected to reach me within seven days. However, more than ten days have passed, and I have neither received the book nor any information regarding the delay.

Body Paragraph

I urgently require this book as my school examinations are approaching. It has been recommended by my English teacher for revision and practice, and several important assignments are based on the exercises contained in it. The unexpected delay has disrupted my preparation.

Body Paragraph

I kindly request you to look into the matter and arrange for the immediate dispatch of the book. If the ordered edition is currently unavailable, I would appreciate it if you could send a similar grammar book of the same standard after informing me. Alternatively, you may also let me know the expected date of availability so that I can decide whether to continue with the order.

Body Paragraph

I hope you will treat this matter as urgent and resolve the issue at the earliest.

Subscription & Signature
Thanking you.

Yours faithfully,

Aman Gupta
