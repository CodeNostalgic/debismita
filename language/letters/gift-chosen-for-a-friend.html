Model Informal Letter

Letter 13: Gift Chosen for a Friend

Structure Highlights
Format: Informal | Word Count: 214 Words | Reading Time: ~1 mins | Standard: ICSE Board Exam
Sender's Address
27, Park Street
Kolkata – 700016
Date
8 July 2026
Salutation
Dear Arjun,
Body Paragraph

I hope you are doing well. Your birthday is just around the corner, and I have been thinking a lot about what gift would be most useful for you. Since I had only a small amount of money to spend, I wanted to choose something meaningful rather than expensive.

Body Paragraph

After visiting several shops, I finally bought you a beautifully designed planner along with a set of colourful sticky notes. I remembered how often you mention forgetting important dates, homework and project deadlines. I felt this gift would help you organise your daily schedule and manage your time more effectively.

Body Paragraph

The planner has sections for daily goals, weekly plans and personal reflections. I am sure it will encourage you to develop the habit of planning your work in advance. The sticky notes can be used to mark important pages in your textbooks or remind you of upcoming tests and assignments. I believe such simple habits can greatly improve one's productivity and confidence.

Body Paragraph

I know this gift may not be very costly, but it has been chosen with great affection and care. I sincerely hope it proves useful and reminds you of our friendship whenever you use it.

Body Paragraph

I look forward to celebrating your birthday with you. Do let me know how you like the gift.

Subscription & Signature
Yours lovingly,

Aman
